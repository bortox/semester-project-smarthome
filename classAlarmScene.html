Detailed Description

Highest priority scene that flashes red on motion detection.

Priority: 255 (Maximum - overrides all other scenes)

Listens to PIR motion sensor events. When motion is detected, flashes all RGBLight devices red (ON/OFF) every 200ms. If no motion for 10 seconds, stops flashing and becomes transparent (allows underlying scenes to show).
