Gets temperature in decicelsius (tenths of degree)

Returns
Temperature * 10 (e.g., 205 = 20.5°C)

LM75 resolution is 0.125°C (1/8), we need 0.1°C (1/10) Formula: decicelsius = (raw_value * 5) >> 2 Explanation: raw * 0.125 * 10 = raw * 1.25 = raw * 5/4
