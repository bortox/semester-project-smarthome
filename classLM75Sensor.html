Detailed Description

LM75 I2C temperature sensor driver (NO FLOATS).

Communicates with LM75 via I2C to read temperature. Returns temperature in decicelsius (tenths of degree) to avoid floating-point operations on AVR.

int16_t LM75Sensor::getValue ( ) const

Gets temperature in decicelsius.

Returns
Temperature × 10 (e.g., 205 = 20.5°C)

LM75 resolution is 0.125°C (1/8), converted to 0.1°C (1/10). Formula: decicelsius = (raw_value × 5) >> 2 Explanation: raw × 0.125 × 10 = raw × 1.25 = raw × 5/4
